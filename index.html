Meet the visionary mind behind the Air Guitar Pro, our founder, Peter Peterson. Inspired by a deep passion for music and a relentless pursuit of innovation, Peter embarked on a transformative journey alongside a dedicated team of engineers. Their mission: to reimagine the guitar-playing experience for musicians worldwide. Over the course of a decade, Peter Peterson and his team tirelessly honed their craft, culminating in a groundbreaking achievement. The result was a fretboard that possessed a remarkable sensitivity to touch, even in the immediate proximity of the fretboard itself. This innovative feature empowers players to strum the instrument without direct contact with the sensing pad, a leap forward in the world of music technology. Harnessing the most advanced technologies available, we've achieved the ability to detect the faintest nuances of each finger strumming, delivering a level of precision and expression that was once only a dream for musicians. At the heart of the Air Guitar's design is a hollow circle within the body, facilitating a seamless playing experience. This elegant design choice ensures that every strum and touch flows effortlessly, allowing you to lose yourself in the music you create.
