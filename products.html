Air Guitar Pro Team

In the realm of musical innovation, our Chief Engineer, Larry Daget, emerged as a visionary leader, assembling a team of brilliant minds to forge the ultimate frontier in stringless advanced sensory guitars. "The guitars can literally feel you; the sensors are that powerful," declares Daget with unwavering confidence. Daget and his team embarked on an extraordinary journey, delving deep into the science of electrical currents and their ability to be sensed by a device. With relentless determination, they unlocked the secrets of detecting touch, even in the broader realm of an area of effect. The result of their groundbreaking work is what they've aptly named "Ghost Strings." These ethereal strings remain invisible and beyond tactile perception, yet the sensors possess the uncanny ability to capture the nuanced movements of every finger on each string. With this remarkable advancement, musicians can now summon a symphony by simultaneously slapping all the strings, unveiling an entirely new dimension of sonic possibilities. Daget's vision, coupled with the unwavering dedication of his team, has forever altered the landscape of music, transforming every touch into an exquisite note and every strum into an expression of artistry.
